How To Play

In this game you have been sentenced to death for being a wizard among the ordinary, and the whole village is after you! Luckily since you're a wizard you have means to deal with them! Your aim is to dispose of the villagers using your love for fireballs. Since you're magic it can be in any direction!

Controls

  • W S A D to move forwards, backwards, left and right respectively.

  • Left click where you want to shoot.

  • Press R to refill your mana.

  • If you figure out how to cheat, you will be punished with eternal gameplay as you cannot die! Say goodbye to that sweet leaderboard entry!

Power-Ups

Heart

When picked up the player regains 30HP.

Surge

When picked up the player shoots fireballs in eight directions for 5s.

Speed Gem

When picked up the player's speed triples for 10s.

Shield

When picked up the player gets a shield which voids all damage for 10s.

Death Plate

When picked up the player instantly kills all other enemies, ending the round.
